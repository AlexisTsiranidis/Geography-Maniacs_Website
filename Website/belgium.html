Belgium
Capital: Brussels
Population: 11,492,641
The Kingdom of Belgium, is a country in Western Europe. It is bordered by the Netherlands to the north, Germany to the east, Luxembourg to the southeast, France to the southwest, and the North Sea to the northwest. It covers an area of 30,689 km2. Major cities are Antwerp, Ghent, Charleroi.
