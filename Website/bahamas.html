Bahamas
Capital: Nassau
Population: 351,461
The Bahamas, is a country within the Lucayan Archipelago of the West Indies in the Atlantic. It consists of more than 700 islands, cays, and islets in the Atlantic Ocean, and is located north of Cuba and NW of the island of Hispaniola and the Turks and Caicos Islands, SE of the US.
