Honduras
Capital: Tegucigalpa
Population: 9,587,522
The Republic of Honduras, is a country in Central America. The republic of Honduras is bordered to the west by Guatemala, to the SW by El Salvador, to the SE by Nicaragua, to the south by the Pacific Ocean at the Gulf of Fonseca, and to the north by the Gulf of Honduras, a large inlet of the Caribbean.
