Iceland is a Nordic island country in the North Atlantic Ocean, is the most sparsely populated country in Europe. Iceland is volcanically and geologically active. Iceland is warmed by the Gulf Stream and has a temperate climate, despite a high latitude just outside the Arctic Circle, most of the archipelago having a polar climate.
